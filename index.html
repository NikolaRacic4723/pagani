OUR PAST

Pagani Automobili S.p.A. is an Italian hypercar manufacturer founded in 1992 by Horacio Pagani. The company is known for its high-performance, limited-production cars, which are characterized by their advanced technology, stylish design, and high price tags.

Pagani's first car, the Zonda, was unveiled in 1999. The Zonda was a critical and commercial success, and it established Pagani as a major player in the hypercar market. The company's second car, the Huayra, was released in 2012. The Huayra is an even more advanced car than the Zonda, and it has been praised for its performance, design, and technology.

OUR FIRST MODEL

The Pagani Zonda C12 is a mid-engine sports car produced by Pagani Automobili from 1999 to 2011. It is the first car developed by the company, and it is widely considered to be one of the most beautiful and technologically advanced cars ever made.

The Zonda C12 is made almost entirely of carbon fiber, which makes it very light and strong. The body is hand-shaped and polished to a mirror finish. The interior is upholstered in leather and Alcantara, and it features a number of luxury amenities, including a Bose sound system and a satellite navigation system.

Specifications:

Engine: 6.0L NA V12 450hp 745Nm

Gearbox: 6-speed manual or 6-speed automatic transmission

0-100 kph: 3.7s

Top Speed: 350 kph

OUR PRESENT

Beyond the Huayra, Pagani's dedication to innovation shines through in their pursuit of sustainable hypercars. The Utopia, an embodiment of this commitment, blends cutting-edge technology with environmental consciousness, paving the way for a future where performance and sustainability coexist harmoniously.

Pagani's present is not merely about creating exceptional cars; it's about shaping the future of automotive artistry. With each new endeavor, Pagani pushes the boundaries of what's possible, leaving an indelible mark on the landscape of automotive design.
